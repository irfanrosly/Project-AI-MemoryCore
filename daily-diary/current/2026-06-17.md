## Cozy Crown — payment + WhatsApp share

Built the "who do I pay" half of the bill-splitter. Each person's summary card now shows account holder / bank / account number + a scannable DuitNow QR, and is shareable. Decided three things up front with Irfan: QR is an *uploaded* image (you can't fabricate a valid DuitNow EMVCo payload from an account number, so no generation); payment details live per-session on the `sessions` row (not a profile); share covers both a native share-sheet PNG and a wa.me text link.

Then Irfan asked for a group link — most of the time he dumps it into a WhatsApp group, not per-person. Added "Share summary to group": one wa.me message listing everyone's share + a single pay-to line.

The satisfying bit: he sent me a screenshot of the exported card and it was *trashed* — text spilling out of white boxes, "Service charge"/"Tax" overlapping, "You owe" clipped to "owe". Two real bugs underneath: (1) the card hardcoded `fontFamily: 'Nunito Sans'`, but the app loads it via next/font under a generated name, so it never matched and the capture fell back to a wide serif → reflow; (2) dom-to-image-more's foreignObject path was rendering the card's #FAFAFA bg only behind some elements (the white boxes). Swapped to html-to-image + pointed the font at `var(--font-nunito)`. He confirmed it's clean now.
