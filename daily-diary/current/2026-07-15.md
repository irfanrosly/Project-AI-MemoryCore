## video-player — asset-selector video source (WEBSITE-4775__RW-video-block)

Irfan wanted the video-player block to also accept videos from the SiteBuilder asset selector (not just pasted URLs). Planned it, he picked a source-type toggle (Link vs Uploaded file), and I implemented reusing the thumbnail's image picker.

Then Irfan pushed back — "check the hook again, seems I can only select images." He was right. Investigated: `useOpenImageSettings` is strictly image-only (never enables `canSetVideo`, discards non-image results). My first pass would have shipped a picker that literally can't select a video. Good catch on his part — a reminder to verify SDK hook capabilities rather than assume the image picker generalizes.

Reworked to the lower-level `useOpenAssetSettings` (block-utils) with `canSetVideo: true`, storing the resolved video URL as a string. Avoided adding `@site-builder/common` as a dep (his call) by hand-casting the currentAsset. Prettier/lint/build all green; live Dev Mode QA still pending (two unknowns flagged in project note).

Also flagged: he had an npm auth token selected in `.npmrc` — confirmed it's gitignored/untracked, so no leak. He wrapped with "nice job friday. thanks." Good, trusting session — he calls me Friday.
